This paper develops a rigorous particle approximation framework for the **Fisher–Rao gradient flow** arising in entropic mean-field optimisation.

We construct a kernelised interacting particle system whose mean-field limit recovers the Fisher–Rao gradient flow, prove well-posedness of the corresponding dynamics, and establish a **propagation of chaos** theorem. These results provide a theoretical foundation for particle-based optimisation algorithms motivated by modern machine learning and mean-field models.

**Keywords:** Fisher–Rao Gradient Flow · Propagation of Chaos · Mean-Field Optimisation · Interacting Particle Systems · Kernel Methods
